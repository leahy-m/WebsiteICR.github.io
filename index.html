          6M Series
          

          

          

          

          


          

          

          

          

          

          
   
          
      
          
         
          
            
          
               
          INSTANT PRODUCTIVITY
           
          THE 6M: POWERFUL. FAST. INTUITIVE.
           
          No fuss, all the power you need and faster – the 6M tractor experience is all about getting things done. These powerful tractors are cost-effective to own, supremely easy to operate from the get-go and will have you zipping along at up to 50 km/h. The 6M Series is built for those who go beyond the ordinary.
          
            
          
         
          
      
          
   
          

          
   
          
      
          
         
          
            
         
            
               
          • 
                  
            
                     
            
                        
            
                        
            6M 230 - 6M 250
            
                        
            HEAVY-DUTY PERFORMERS
            

            With stellar on-road performance and a rock-solid, comfortable ride even in heavy-duty applications thanks to their 100 mm longer wheelbase and 2.05 m tyres, the 6M Series Xtra Large Frame models give you powerful and efficient versatility.
            
                     
            
                     
            
                  
            
               
            • 
               
          • 
                  
            
                     
            
                        
            
                        
            6M 180 - 6M 240
            
                        
            THE POWER TO PERFORM
            

            When your jobs are big, numerous, and tough, these 6M Series tractors are for you. As an arable farmer, you will love their 2,800 mm wheelbase and oversized tyres that bring all power to the ground with maximum efficiency. Performance-oriented contractors also favour these mid-spec tractors for their top on-road performance.
            
                     
            
                     
            
                  
            
               
            • 
               
          • 
                  
            
                     
            
                        
            
                        
            6M 145 - 6M 185
            
                        
            SOLID POWER
            

            These 6-cylinder 6M Series tractors deliver a solid and comfortable ride in the field and on the road. Their ideal blend of agility, power, and durability makes these 6M tractors robust and reliable mid-spec performers in heavier fieldwork.
            
                     
            
                     
            
                  
            
               
            • 
               
          • 
                  
            
                     
            
                        
            
                        
            6M 130 – 6M 150
            
                        
            THE ALL-ROUNDERS
            

            The power to pull more in the field and handle bigger implements, agile enough for loader work, and faster on the road – experience the versatility of these compact, high-horsepower 4-cylinder 6M tractors.
            
                     
            
                     
            
                  
            
               
            • 
               
          • 
                  
            
                     
            
                        
            
                        
            6M 95 – 6M 125
            
                        
            COMPACT BUILD, AGILE PERFORMANCE
            

            Their short wheelbase and compact build predestine these 6M Series models for front loader work even in the tightest spaces. With a mighty payload of up to 4.7 tons, the high-achieving mid-spec 6M tractors also won’t break a sweat with heavy implements. With a top speed of 50 km/h, a 6M tractor gets you where you need to be fast.
